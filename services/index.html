Here’s the process I follow when working on a project:

  1. Brief - We talk about what you want, your time frame, and your budget.
  2. Agreement - You’ll sign my freelance agreement and then I’ll get to work.
  3. Initial Payment - The first part is paid upfront based on my quote.
  4. Creation - I put in the work to design and build your project.
  5. Revisions - You’ll be in the loop so you can ask for minor changes.
  6. Sign off - Once complete, you’ll sign off on the project.
  7. Final Payment - You’ll send your final payment and receive your project.
