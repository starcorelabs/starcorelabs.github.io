Salesforce Admin Training

2019-01-22

A few months ago I was talking with a friend about how I wanted to change my career.

I am considering a few different options; weighting the pros and cons of each.

He said Salesforce Administration is in demand and recommended I learn it.
At the very least it would be a great stepping stone until I decide my next career path.

I took his advice and started by watching a video course: Salesforce Certified Administrator (aka 201) 2018

After completing the video course I did some online research and learned two things:

  1. I needed more experience with the new Lightning Experience user interface. Because the video course covered the classic interface.
  2. I needed a way to “prove” I had learned the skills needed to be a Salesforce Administrator.

So I signed up for the Trailhead: Salesforce Admin Beginner

As of this post, I’m currently at 74% with the course. I have 2 hours 20 mins of training left to complete it.

Learning Salesforce Administration requires a different way of thinking.
Understanding the requirements of the organization and users who will interact with Salesforce.

Along with knowing the platform and the logic of setting up Apps, with forms, and fields that best serve the users needs.

So far I’m enjoying learning Salesforce. Once I complete this course I plan on pursuing other courses on Trailhead.
