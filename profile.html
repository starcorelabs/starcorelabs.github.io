Problems and Limitations

I'm a high-functioning autistic. Being autistic means I am biologically wired differently. I see things differently and I'm motivated by different things. Whatever you think I might want is only your own subconscious projecting your desires on to me. You are wrong. Stop it.

I process sensory information differently. From my point of view, when I'm in a crowded room, all the sounds blend together into one sound. It takes a lot of focus for me to listen through the noise. This is why I prefer silence or interacting in small groups.

Most autistic people have horrible social skills. I've spent time studying and learning how to interact with people. It was not easy and sometimes I do not pick up on the subtle social cues other people put out.

This is not something I'm doing on purpose to be a jerk. I'm simply unable to pick up those signals.

By nature I am very introverted. I prefer to spend most of my time alone. I have taught myself how to interact with other people. Sometimes when I'm around people I talk too much and annoy others without meaning too. When I choose to interact my goal is to make people laugh. This because I'm practicing my comedy skills and trying to improve them. If you don't feel like talking just tell me. I'll will leave you alone.

I have blood-sugar problems. Basically I do not eat sweets of any kind because they make me feel sick. Don't worry about it. I don't miss sweet stuff. I have adapted myself to a very specific diet that helps me balance my blood sugar. Do not offer me food or sweets.

When my blood sugar goes too high or low it causes me migraine headaches and my whole body hurts. I cannot focus or think straight. I get very impatient and irritable. You might take it like I'm angry or upset. It is just my fucked up bio-chemstry.

Do not offer me words to help me feel better. My issue is not mental or emotional. You are only wasting time and it will only irritate me more. The only things that work is time, food, and sleep. Usually I just have to wait for my body to balance out my blood sugar.

My anxiety and depresson are directly linked with my blood sugar issues. When my blood sugar is balanced I'm fine.

While I do appreciate the offer. I do not know how the food you're offering will affect me. I don't want to feel like crap. Do us both a favor and don't offer me any food. Just accept that I know what what works for me. I'm 40 years old and I know how to take care of myself.

I do not like conflict, debate, being judged, being told what to do, what to think, how to live, other people's unrealistic expectations put on me, immaturity, and/or hostility of any kind. If I don't ask for your advice then don't offer it. If you choose to judge me. I will use my well trained observation skills and point out all of your flaws. I'm getting pretty good at it. Don't tempt me.

When people are rude or harsh I stop interacting with them. I do not forgive or forget. If you are rude or disrespect me once we are done. That is how I am wired. I have better things to do with my time.
