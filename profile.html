What I Do

Design is my career focus.

I'm skilled with Windows, Mac and Linux. But I prefer Windows when working in an office environment.

I use Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD, Gimp, Inkscape, and Balsamiq to create wireframes and mockups of web applications. As well as, graphics for logos, business cards, email marketing, and social media.

To see a small example of my work check out my portfolio.

What I Want

  • A design job where I spend the majority of my time sitting at a desk using software to wireframe and prototype web and mobile application layouts.
  • I'm open to creating graphics for logos, branding, and marketing.
  • I prefer a positive company culture where internal departments cooperative with each other.
  • A place that values building lasting relationships with employees. While respecting their skills, experience, and differences.
  • Growth minded managers who are solution focused and work with the team.
  • Stakeholders who clearly define business needs before creating projects.
  • Projects based on verified customer data and planned out appropriately.
  • Time managament methodologies based on the needs of the project.
  • Streamlined tasks provided by project managers who ask the right questions.
  • Appropriate responsibilities and realistic expectations of progress based on the existing skills of the team.
  • Competive salary based on industry standards.
