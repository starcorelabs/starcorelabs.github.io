Support Packages

I only offer limited remote support for Desktops and Laptops running Windows 7 or Windows 10. My remote support service requires an active high-speed internet connection.

If you are not online or your internet speed is too slow for a remote connection. I suggest you take your computer to a local tech support shop.

I do not offer support, advice, or even suggestions for any other devices.

This includes all Apple computers, tablets, and phones. As well as all other tablets, phones, and other electronics.

For those devices you’ll need to contact the manufacturer or a local repair shop that offers support for that device.
