  • Video production or editing
  • Coding or programming

Question 2

Q: How do subscriptions work?
A: After you subscribe you'll get an email with links to Trello and Discord.
Then you can start adding UI design tasks in whatever priority you want.

Question 3

Q: How do payments work?
A: All subscription payments are made in advance for the next 30 day billing cycle.
Per project payments start with the base price and increases depending on the complexity of the project.

Question 4

Q: How many design tasks can I request?
A: You can add 60 tasks per month.

Question 5

Q: What if I don't like the results or want to make changes?
A: No problem. You get 60 revisions per month to make changes.

Question 6

Q: How do I request user interface designs?
A: Through Trello.

Question 7

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at anytime.
Send me a request through Trello or Discord.

Question 8

Q: What if I want my money back.
A: Unfortunately, due to the nature of creative work we do not offer any refunds.
