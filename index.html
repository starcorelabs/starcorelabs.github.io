Hack Your Education

Joey shows us how he got a full Bachelors Degree in Software Development for only $8,000, without going into debt.

The following was written by Joey:

Western Govenors University is based in Utah. http://www.wgu.edu. Inadvertently, I received a scholarship from the university.

Prior to enrollment, I used the free Khan Academy to prep for college courses.

I also used Lynda.com to help make my text book skills more marketable.

Here are some other resources that might help you as well:

  • Coursera.org - free fully acreddited college courses.
  • Straighterline.com - fully acredited courses without having into enroll in college.

While in School, all the WGU books you need are available for free in their E library. However, before I was in the school I bought all my IT books used on ebay and amazon. Some I borrowed for free from the local library. Using this method, I was able to get 10 IT certifications for less than $1000. My bachelors degree was obtained in two years. Instead of the traditional four.

After the scholarship, that came out to only costing me $10,000. Moreover, most IT companies with reimburse you for your degree after 1 year of employment. So, the second year of my education was not paid by me. School costed $6000 per year, I said the total cost was $10,000, so I only paid, $4,000 for a fully accredited bachelors degree plus $1,000 for the certs I bought prior to enrollment.

Bootstrap 3 Overview

While I was working as front-end developer for a large company I was tasked with teaching the other developers Bootstrap 3.

Bootstrap is a CSS library used for responsive web development. This means each page in your site will look good on smart phones, tablets, and desktop computers. No need to create multiple pages for each device type.
