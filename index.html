What is Starcore Labs?

We are a web design and development firm located in Indianapolis, IN. We specialize in making custom bootstrap-based mobile friendly themes.

Designed for you

We build the design based on what you want. Combined with industry standards. We consider every aspect of your theme to be important.

Time saver

Projects

DeepSpace.css

While working for Celadon I kept having to create small spacing classes to deal with padding, margin, and borders. Out of frustration I combined all of the most often repeated classes into a single library. I named it DeepSpace.css.

Driver Portal - For Celadon Trucking

While working for Celadon Trucking I was part of the project to make an Enterprise-Level web application. The truck drivers needed an easier way to view their settlement sheets on their smart phones. I brought Bootstrap 3 in to build the responsive interface for the site.

Fargone Productions - Wordpress Theme

A friend of mine runs his own multimedia company in Chicago. He had gotten so busy over the last couple of years that he pretty much forgot about his website. I designed a new streamlined theme for him. I am currently 60% finished with it.
