Online Communities for Graphic Designers

Connecting with other Graphic Designers and Artists will give you a group of like-minded individuals to learn from and talk with.

I've compiled a list of online resources where you can join communities for Graphic Designers.

Online Communities

  • Reddit Graphic Design
  • Dribbble
  • Behance
  • Deviant Art
  • Design Snack
  • Design Bump
  • Design Float
  • Logo Design Love
  • Coroflot
  • Graphic Design Forum
  • GDUSA.com
  • Aiga.org
  • How Design
  • All Graphic Design
  • Design Forums

Take some time and explore each of these resources. Read through the forums, posts, and comments on these sites to get a feel for how the memebers interact. This will give you a better idea of which community is right for you.
