A Typical Day

2021-11-29

This is a typical day in my life as a UX/UI Designer.
Some days are busier than others.
I am more productive with less meetings.

9:00am to 10:00am

  • Stretch and try to wake up.
  • Eat breakfast.
  • Turn on Linux laptop and check personal emails and messages.

10:00am to 12:00pm

  • Login to the company provided Windows 10 Pro PC.
  • Set Teams chat status to "Busy".
  • Check Outlook, Teams, and Trello for updates.

  • Reply to any urgent emails or messages and prepare for any meetings.
  • Do research and work on assigned design tasks in Adobe XD.

12:00pm to 1:00pm

  • Lunch

1:00 to 4:00pm

  • More design work in Adobe XD.
  • Maybe have a meeting regarding one of the three projects I'm working on.

4:00pm to 4:30pm

  • Every Tuesday we have a video Team Meeting through Teams Chat.

4:30pm to 5:00pm

  • Finish up design tasks, replay to any emails, and log my time.
  • Set Teams chat status to "Offline".
  • Shutdown work PC.
