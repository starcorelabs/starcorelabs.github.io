Process

Below is the 10 step process I use to gather information and organize resources. Then design and deliver solutions in a professional and timely manner.

  1. Brief - A set of questions used to give me a detailed undestanding of your business, customers, project, and goal.
  2. Agreement - A document that clearly defines: what, when, and how things get done.
  3. Initial Payment - Based on the quote I provide. Half of the payment is made upfront before work begins.
  4. Research - Using the information in the Brief as a starting point. I research to design creative solutions to solve your problem.
  5. Creation - Using the Brief and my Research I begin designing the graphics that will fulfill your request.
  6. Revisions - Once I have created a few variations of the requested graphics I will send you samples for approval and posssible revisions if needed.
  7. Production - Once all revisions have been made and you are happy with the final version. I will create the high-quality production files. Sending you a sample for final approval.
  8. Sign Off - When you are ready to recieve the production files, you'll sign off on the final version in the Agreement.
  9. Final Payment - The last half of the agreed payment must be sent before receiving your Deliverables.
  10. Deliverables - After confirmation of final payment a zipped folder with all source files and rendered graphics will be sent via email.
