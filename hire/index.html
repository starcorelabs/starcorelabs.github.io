Process

When working with clients I follow these steps:

  1. I ask questions and listen to understand.
  2. Am I right for your project?
  3. Are we solving the right problem?
  4. What is the real goal?
  5. We review my contract together and clarify time frame, scope of service, mile stones, and pricing.
  6. Once the contract is signed and the first payment is made, then I get started.  

Value Based Pricing

Each project is unique and so is it's value to you and your customers.

Pricing for a project depends on many variables:

  • Your vision
  • The current market

  • Project complexity
  • UX Design

  • Time frame
