Process

Over the years I've developed a streamlined process to work with clients.

Before any agreements are made or contracts are signed I hold an informational meeting with a potiental client. This is where they explain their product idea and goals.

My goal during the first meeting is to figure out if I'm the right designer for their project. I'm also listening for indicators that they understand the services I offer and have the money to invest. This tells me they respect my time, experience, and skills.

Here's the questions I ask:

  • Have you shopped around for other Designers?
  • Am I right for your project?
  • Do you understand the services I offer?
  • Who is the target audience and what problem(s) are we solving for them?
  • What research and resource gathering have you done?
  • What is your budget for this project?
  • What is the timeframe of the project?

Follow Up Steps

If I agree to take on a client we have a second meeting to hammer out the business aspects:

  • We review my contract together and clarify time frame, scope of services, mile stones, and pricing.
  • After the contract is signed and the first payment is made, then I get started.  

Pricing

I offer two pricing models for my services.

  • Project-Based Pricing
  • Value-Based Pricing

Note: I do not offer hourly based pricing.

Project-Based Pricing

This model works best for small UX/UI projects or simple graphical assets. This is a flat fee for the design work I do on the project. It must be fully paid in advance before I start working. The total price is determined by the complexity, effort, and time it takes to create assets.

Value-Based Pricing

This model is best for medium to large projects. Each project is unique and so is it's value to you and your customers.

I start with the projected revenue this project will generate for your company and then estimate 30% of that. Then I add on the complexity, effort, and time it takes to create those assets.  

Payments

Value-Based project payments are split up into two parts. Half upfront and half upon completion of the project.

After full payment has been made. Then the asset files will be zipped and emailed to the client.

Note: I do not store client project files after completion.

At the moment I only accept payments through: PayPal and Square.
