FAQ

Here is a list of the frequently asked questions I get. If you have a question that is not answered here feel free to send me an email and I'll respond asap.

  • Q: What is Starcore Labs?
  • A: Starcore Labs LLC is a company based in Indianapolis, IN that specializes in responsive front-end development. I offer my services to businesses that either need to make their web site responsive or want to create a new reusable responsive template for their project.
  • Q: How long have you been a Web Developer?
  • A: 8 years total. 5 years as a freelance developer and 3 years in corporate environments.
  • Q: How much do you charge
  • A: That all depends on the complexity of the project and the time frame involved. Give me a call and tell me about your projcet. I'm sure we can work together to create a solution that works for you.
  • Q: Do you work as a freelancer or as an employee?
  • A: I'm comfortable doing either. I have my own contract if needed. I can work under W2 if my client feels more comfortable that way.
  • Q: Do you work on-site or remote?
  • A: I prefer working remotely as I am more productive. However, I willing to travel up to 20 minutes from my location to work on-site if needed.
  • Q: What web sites and projects have you worked on?
  • A: My portfolio will give you a break down of all the projects I've worked on to date. You can check my Github repo to view examples of the code I've written.
  • Q: I see you were in Technical Support before switching to Web Development. What made you switch careers?
  • A: I originally went to TechSkills vocational school to earn certifications toward web development. After school I was fortunate to obtain a job doing technical support until I could launch my web development career.
  • Q: How much freelance work have you done? How many hours a week?
  • A: I have done a handful of projects over the years. Mostly small Wordpress sites. Only 4 to 6 hours of work for each project. The contract varies for each client and company.
  • Q: Do you have a degree?
  • A: No. I have three techincal certifications: A+, Network+, MCTS.
  • Q: How strong is your JavaScript? Have you used any Frameworks or Libraries like Node, Backbone, and/or Angular?
  • A: I am a JavaScript Beginner. JavaScript has not been a major component in the last few projects I was part of.
  • Q: Have you done any Object-Oriented JavaScript Development?
  • A: Not yet. I am still learning the basics of JavaScript.
  • Q: What tools and languages do you feel you are most proficient in?
  • A: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap. I prefer using Brackets code editor.
  • Q: Can you use Bootstrap independent, or in place of, a JavaScript Library?
  • A: Yes it can be used independent because Bootstrap is not a JavaScript library. It is a CSS front-end rapid development framework that has some jQuery optional elements. It gives developers the ability to quickly create web pages that will scale based on the screen size of the device being used.
  • Q: Are the pages you make with Bootstrap cross platform and cross browser capable?
  • A: Yes, Bootstrap provides that out of the box. I always test my work in all major browsers.
  • Q: Have you used Phone Gap or Cordova?
  • A: I am aware of them but I have not used them. Those platforms are out of scope for a front-end developer like myself.
  • Q: Have you used C#, .Net, PHP, Java, or Ruby?
  • A: No, those technologies run on the back-end and are out of scope for front-end development.
  • Q: What skills and technologies are you the most interested in improving upon or learning?
  • A: I’m currently working through a book on JavaScript. I have taken a few online courses to improve my JavaScript skills. Unfortunately, it is taking me a long time to learn how to program in JavaScript.
  • Q: How comfortable are you with writing HTML entirely by hand?
  • A: I write HTML and CSS by hand all the time. I prefer it.
  • Q: What web browser do you use?
  • A: All of them. My default browser is Google Chrome. I really like their development tools the best.
