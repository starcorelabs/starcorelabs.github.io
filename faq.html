FAQ

Here is a list of the frequently asked questions I get. If you have a question that is not answered here feel free to send me an email and I'll respond asap.

  • Q: What is Starcore Labs?
  • A: Starcore Labs LLC is a company based in Indianapolis, IN that specializes in web development for small business.
  • Q: How long have you been a Web Developer?
  • A: 8 years total. 5 years as a freelance developer and 3 years in corporate environments.
  • Q: How much do you charge
  • A: I offer three packages.
    • $60.00 an hour for larger projects or anything that involves eCommerce.
    • $400.00 flat for small to medium projects.
    • $100.00 for return clients who have questions or need minor fixes.
  • Q: Do you work as a freelancer or as an employee?
  • A: I am a freelancer with my own LLC. I prefer to do business under my own contract.
  • Q: Do you work on-site or remote?
  • A: I prefer working remotely as I am more productive.
