About Me

Michael Douglas

I am a UX Designer / Front-End Developer located in Indianapolis, Indiana.

My career focus is UX Design. But, I'm open to doing some Front-End Development.

For UX Design I use Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Gimp, Inkscape, Balsamiq, and Figma to create wireframes and mockups of web applications. As well as, graphics for logos, business cards, email marketing, and social media.

For Front-End Development I use GIT to manage code revisions, Atom editor to write HTML, CSS/SCSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery code. I also use Node with Gulp to automate converting SCSS into CSS for deployment.

I improve my skills through books, video courses, and hands on experiments.

When not working I can be found reading comic books, playing video games or watching Comedy, Fantasy, or Sci-Fi movies.

Ideal Role

  • A UX Design position where I spend the majority of my time wireframing and prototyping web and mobile application layouts.
  • A company, with a positive culture and cooperative internal departments, that values building lasting relationships with employees. While respecting their skills, experience, and differences.
  • Growth minded managers who are solution focused and work with the team.
  • Stakeholders who clearly define business needs before creating projects.
  • Projects based on verified customer data and planned out appropriately.
  • Time managament methodologies based on the needs of the project.
  • Streamline tasks provided by project managers who ask the right questions.
  • Appropriate responsibilities and realistic expectations of progress based on existing skills of the design team.
  • Competive salary based on industry standards.
