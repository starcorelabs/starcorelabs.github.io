Aventiv

2023-03-20

Summary

I was the lead UI Designer for the Aventiv "AllPaid" brand.
I also designed user interface solutions for five other company projects.

Due to a non-discloser agreement, I'm unable to display any Aventiv proprietary content on this portfolio.

Company

Aventiv headquarters is based in Texas.

Here's what their website says:

"Aventiv exists to empower both individuals and communities for better futures. Through the advancement of technology, we strengthen bonds, improve safety, and create opportunities for those who need them most."

They own and manage four brands:

  • AllPaid
  • JPay
  • Securus Monitoring
  • Securus Technologies

Role

Example of Adobe XD

I collaborated with the project manager to create UI solutions while working within project specifications, the corporate style guide, legal restrictions, and technical limitations.

I used Adobe XD to create deliverables like flowcharts, site maps, user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes for mobile and web applications.

These deliverables were used by developers as blueprints to build working applications.

Team

The majority of the company works remotely and is spread across many different states. Because of this, I've only interacted with them through Teams chat and virtual meetings.

Everyone on the team was kind and professional. I enjoyed collaborating with them on different projects.

Projects

Using Adobe XD I made UI Designs for the following six projects:

  • AllPaid
  • AdminCenter
  • GovPayNet
  • GovPayNow
  • JPay
  • Proview (Internal Project)

Mass Layoff Chaos

On Tuesday, March 7th, 2023 at 11:00 AM EST Aventiv held an "All Hands Meeting".

Employees were informed the company would be restructuring, which lead to the elimination of 18% of the workforce. I was part of the unlucky group.

While I will miss the team I worked with for the last two years. I'm optimistic about the future and look forward to my next adventure.
